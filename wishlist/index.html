Seestar S50

$499.00

This telescope is not good for regular stargazing, but it is very good at astrophotography, especially for its price. One thing I don't like about this is that it takes away the entire process. The solution would be to have this telescope for pictures, and a manual one for stargazing.

Askar 71F

$599.00

I think it's time to move on to a better telescope than the one I've had for 10 years. While I couldn't do astrophotography really easily like the seestar-s50 allows for, it's a different experience to be looking through a lens and seeing deep space objects in real time, setting up several parts to take a picture, instead of just only waiting on a picture. This telescope is adjustable and allows for very good views of both planets and deep space objects for a really good price. This is the quality you would expect on a telescope worth over $1000.
